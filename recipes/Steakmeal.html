Steak Meal

Description

Generally, people appreciate meat over chicken

The epitomy of meat may be steak

So today, we'll be mixing steak with some mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of steak
  • 2 Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak sauce

Steps

  1. Boil peeled potatoes in pot for 30 minutes
  2. Mash potatoes and put aside
  3. Marinate steak in olive oil
  4. Add salt and pepper to entire surface of steak
  5. Cook steak on stove for 8 minutes, flipping once halfway through
  6. Place steak on plate with mashe potatoes
  7. Serve with steak sauce
