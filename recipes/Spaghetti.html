Spaghetti

Description

Today, we will diving into Italian Cuisine.

This journey will captivate the senses

Not really... heres the ingredients list though:

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Spaghetti
  • Tomato Sauce (250 ml)
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  2. Add salt to the mix
  3. Add Spaghetti in and cook for 7-9 minutes or until cooked
  4. Drain Spaghetti
  5. Return Spaghetti to the pot
  6. Add tomato sauce, oregano to the pot and mix
  7. Move to a plate and add Paremesan Cheese
